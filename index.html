About

Academic profile

I am a PhD student in the Department of Cyber Security Engineering at George Mason University, working with Dr. Anomadarshi Barua in the System Design and Security Lab.

My work sits at the intersection of generative AI, speech and audio signal processing, and cyber-physical system security. I develop models that reconstruct intelligible high-frequency audio from ultra-low-frequency vibration and pressure-sensor data, with applications in system security, assistive technologies, and side-channel analysis.

Research

Research interests

Audio reconstruction

Speech bandwidth extension, speech super-resolution, and intelligible audio recovery from constrained sensor data.

Cyber-physical security

Sensor side channels, acoustic leakage, HVAC systems, vibration channels, and embedded-system security.

Generative AI

Deep learning systems for signal restoration, low-resource language processing, and practical AI-assisted security research.
